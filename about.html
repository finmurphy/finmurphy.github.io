About Dohub

Dohub is the brainchild of Founder Finbarr Murphy who came across a number of unmet needs whilst project managing global teams developing electronic trading platforms for investment banks across London.

As well as identifying problems for large-scale companies through his work in electronic marketplaces, Finbarr is also a director of an online wine merchant. In the day to day running of the store, Finbarr also noticed many inefficiencies in communication with suppliers, retailers, coordinating deliveries and logistics. He came to the realisation that many of the same communication and management issues existed when negotiating between multiple different parties in the supply chain, as for communicating between different departments and teams in a global enterprise.

The solution is Dohub - a project management platform that allows users to break a project down into individual tasks, allowing a variety of freelancers, suppliers, contractors or even internal teams to bid or the parts of a project they specialise in, and project managers to have an exceedingly simple dashboard from which to manage the overall project.

Whilst Dohub was born out of a search for ways to make cross-team collaboration and communication easier in large scale projects, Finbarr realised there were many applications and problems that could be solved for small businesses, freelancers and global enterprises alike.

Dohub enables a business to work with a community of other small to mid-size verified businesses to efficiently meet objectives through collaboration, while working together to fuel growth by finding additional customers and taking on larger projects.

Dohub offers businesses a common platform to manage their projects, matching requests with tailored quotes from a vetted and 'smart sourced' list of service providers, saving time and money.
