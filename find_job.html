Find a job with Dohub


Dohub can help you find work - whether you are a freelancer, contractor, consultant or business. The Dohub marketplace has a variety of projects and tasks that you can search and bid for.


Find freelance work


Freelancers can use Dohub to find new clients, with the unique ability to bid specifically for the tasks they have the expertise for and interest in. Unlike other freelance marketplaces, freelancers do not have to miss out on jobs or projects because they are one skill short of the set. Instead, freelancers can collaborate with those that DO have the right expertise (or even bring in other freelancers they work with) to complete a whole project. With Dohub, freelancers can find much more specific and appropriate work.

To find freelance work, simple browse the Dohub project marketplace and bid for those that are right for you. It's free to sign up!


Find contract work


Dohub is a fantastic platform for contractors to find work by building on the projects or tasks that match their expertise. From small businesses to large enterprises, Dohub has a range of projects and tasks available for external contractors. Dohub provides the simplest and easiest platform for businesses of all sizes to "smart source" the work they need, and only the work they need.

Sign up for free to browse projects and tasks available and find contract work now.


Find new clients


Consultants, freelancers, contractors and B2B companies of all shapes and sizes can find new clients and search for jobs in the Dohub marketplace. Dohub provides the most unique set of jobs as they are all task based, allowing members to find exactly which tasks they want, providing an incredibly efficient and effective talent matching service.
