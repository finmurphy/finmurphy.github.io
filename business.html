Dohub is project collaboration and communication made easy.

1. Post your project from the Dohub dashboard

  • Project planning made simple - overall goals and subtasks defined in minutes.
  • Easily manage projects with multiple teams, managers, freelancers and employees.
  • Project progress and billing at a glance.

2. Break project down into key tasks.

  • Small businesses, freelancers and subject matter experts can bid for the indiviual task.

  • Contractors, freelancers, other deparments and other companies bid for each tasks.
